Business rules for this EMS client:
- Nevada Ground EMS should not be modeled as traditional “contracting.”
- Use service agreement / protocol alignment language instead of contract language.
- This client can only pick up patients inside the jurisdiction of the City of Las Vegas.
- We need to track facility pain points, current transport volume, payer mix, and current provider relied on.
- Important contact roles include Charge Nurse and Director of Nursing.
- “Active Account” should be based on consistency of use, not signature.
- Recommended active rule:
  - Active = at least 1 qualified transport request per week for 4 consecutive weeks
  - Fallback = 4+ qualified transports in a rolling 30-day period

     discharge workflow / call protocol / operational agreement being aligned

     facility is ready to send transports and staff know the process

     account has demonstrated consistent transport usage
   - Expansion:
     volume is growing or additional units / departments / campuses are being targeted
